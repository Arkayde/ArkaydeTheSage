About

Arkayde The Sage is web developer, DJ, beatmaker, and producer located in Southern Virginia. Making beats since the age of 15, Arkayde The Sage sports a dark and unique hip-hop sound that you won't find anywhere else.

Unique Music

Arkayde The Sage offers unique beats with a dark and emo flavor. The music, often inspired by a depth of emotions, sports not only funk influnces but also punk rock, classic hip-hop, and the percussion that is the heart and soul of the DMV (D.C., Maryland, and Virginia).

Affordable Pricing

Arkayde The Sage prices all of their non-exclusive mp3 beats at an affordable $10 so that artists are met with consistency, quality, and access to the music they need. Arkayde The Sage also utilizes BeatStars, an active music platform for safely and securely purchasing and leasing beats.
